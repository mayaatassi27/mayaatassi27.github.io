I work in the McMahon Cosmology Lab on absolute polarization-angle calibration for the Simons Observatory Small Aperture Telescopes. The goal is to determine the telescope’s polarization angle with very high precision by combining detector measurements with a well-characterized calibration geometry.

My work focuses on a photogrammetry-based geometry pipeline, from image calibration and 3D reconstruction to extracting the plate pose in the telescope coordinate frame and propagating uncertainties into the electromagnetic model.

**Tools:** Python, Zephyr, OpenCV, photogrammetry, uncertainty analysis
