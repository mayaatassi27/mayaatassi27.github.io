As part of the COOL-LAMPS collaboration, I work on precision photometry of strongly lensed images using multi-band imaging data. This project begins with careful aperture photometry and moves toward more model-based approaches, including tools such as GALFIT, to better characterize lensed sources.

My work focuses on validating image calibrations, propagating photometric uncertainties, and preparing measurements for downstream scientific analysis such as spectral energy distribution fitting and lens/source-plane inference.

This project has been a good way to connect careful measurement, uncertainty handling, and astronomical interpretation in a strong-lensing context.

**Tools:** Python, photometry, uncertainty propagation, astronomical imaging analysis, GALFIT
